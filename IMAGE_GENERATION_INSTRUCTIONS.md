- Photography style: real food/event photography, not illustration
- Lighting: warm, natural, golden-hour or soft indoor light
- Mood: inviting, premium, slightly editorial
- Colour grading: warm tones, slight desaturation of shadows
- No text, logos, or watermarks in any image
- All featured images: landscape orientation, approximately 1600 x 900 px
