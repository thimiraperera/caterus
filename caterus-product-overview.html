Caterus · Product Overview & Feature Spec · v0.1

What we're building.

Caterus is a managed marketplace where people find, compare and book vetted Melbourne caterers for a specific event — and pay in one place. We launch curated (we control every listing), then open self-serve caterer accounts once supply outgrows manual management. Payment always flows through the platform.

Platform: Responsive web app (mobile-first) Users: Customer · Caterer · Admin Payments: Stripe Connect Model: Managed marketplace
The three users

Who uses it, and for what.

Customer

Wants catering for an event. Searches, compares, books and pays — and reorders next time.

Caterer

Lists their business, receives bookings and gets paid. Curated by us first; self-managed later.

Admin (us)

Vets caterers, manages listings, matches & confirms orders, oversees payments and quality.

Build scope

What launches first.

Phase 1 · MVP — Curated

The thin, lovable platform

  • Customer: search, browse curated caterers, build & book an order, pay via Stripe
  • Admin: manage listings, vet caterers, confirm orders, handle payouts
  • No caterer logins yet — we control quality and the relationship
  • Booking can be confirmed concierge-style behind the scenes
Phase 2 · Scale — Hybrid

Hand caterers the keys (under our gate)

  • Caterer accounts: self-serve profiles, menus & live availability
  • Still vetted & approved by us before going live
  • Reviews, loyalty credits, 1-tap reorder for customers
  • Promoted listings & analytics — the higher-margin revenue
